Chapter 1:

Hesitance


You've travelled so far, over such a long period, it's difficult to remember where you came from. Now this! A wall that stretches in all directions seemingly forever. You put so much effort into your journey, to be hampered like this. Bricks, rows and stacks of bricks.
No. You refuse to let this be it. It's just a barrier and barriers can be broken down. You raise your hands, clenched fists, thrust your arms and hope for the best.
